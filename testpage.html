Understand your Audience

Leverage the combined power of your data and our tools to better understand your audience and its journey as it engages with you.

People are more than the sum of their attributes. We collect every possible signal from each member of your audience by combining behavioral, transactional, attributional, communal, and consumer data to map out their journeys and find the hidden opportunities that will boost your acquisition, conversion, and activation rates.

Test your Stratedgy

Test each step of your audience journeys – from acquisition to conversion and activation – to identify strategies that drive major improvements.

We are strong believers that everything that can be tested should be tested. We will help you find winning strategies by deploying randomized experiments and A/B tests that measure the impact of every action you take along your audience journeys. Once we identify what works - both generally and for key audience segments - we will help you implement them in your digital and offline programs.

Optimize Outreach

Tailor your engagement with each person - including your message, branding, channels, and frequency - to maximize your impact and cost-effectiveness.

Whether you’re a small non-profit or a Fortune 100 company, the way you engage your audience can have a large impact on your bottom line. We deliver a unique omni-channel, omni-message approach to tailor exactly the right outreach to the right person - all while operating at scale. Our proprietary tools help clients allocate budgets across digital and offline channels and then prioritize those who should receive outreach through each channel - including social media, banner and search ads, pre-roll video, broadcast and cable TV, streaming TV, terrestrial and streaming radio, mail, and phone.

Scale your Success

Implement automated segmentation, validation, and machine learning to create a seamless optimization loop that continuously improves your outcomes.

Innovative data science requires a strong and agile analytics technology. We deliver tools to automatically move segments and models into production, and use machine learning to detect new trends that require attention. We also help our clients make the right technology and manpower investments, and put the right processes in place, to operate at the scale they need to succeed.
