Revolutionizing Cross-Channel Outreach

The advertising landscape is more challenging than ever. Media consumption is fragmenting, cord cutters are an increasingly large segment, and digital is no longer a single medium.

Understanding your audience and building a single media plan that holistically reaches it is critical to future success.

Born in the testing grounds of politics, upX marries innovative upift models and a sophisticated cross-channel engine to forecast the optimal media budget distribution for reaching the most influenceable targets in the most cost-effective way.

But it doesn’t end there. Because upX is driven by person-level data, it produces target universes for addressable outreach, and identifies the best TV shows and channels for programmatic buys.

How is upX different?

PERSON LEVEL

Instead of basing targeting on broad generalizations, upX is based on the unique attributes of each person.

GEARED FOR UPLIFT

Even advanced tools are geared for traditional audiences and targets. upX targets those most likely to be moved by your particular ad.

PRE-SPEND

Our predictive uplift models allow you to determine your best media plan before spending your first dollar.

FAST AND AGILE

The online upX platform gives you full control to customize your own buy and get results within hours.

ONLINE AND OFFLINE

Whether you're running prerolls on social, sending mail, or making programmatic buys on TV, radio, or online, upX has the answer.

DOZENS OF SCENARIOS

The right media plan for January is the wrong one for April. You can use upX to forecast how cost and market changes impact your buys.
